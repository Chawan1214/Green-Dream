    About Us

    Mission

    GREEN DREAM is dedicated to preserving the natural beauty of Kurdistan and Iraq through education, advocacy, and action. Our mission is to foster a deep love for the environment and empower individuals and communities to take meaningful steps towards a greener, healthier, and more sustainable future

    Vision

    A Greener Tomorrow, A Brighter Future for Kurdistan and Iraq






    who we are &

    What We Do

    Green Dream takes a multifaceted approach to environmental service in Kurdistan and Iraq: Advocacy and Awareness, Community Engagement, Youth Empowerment, Environmental Education, and Sustainable Projects. We are committed to raising awareness, engaging communities, empowering youth, providing education, and supporting sustainable projects for a more environmentally resilient Kurdistan and Iraq.

    Our Activities

    Tree Planting Campaigns: Rejuvenating greenery across the region by organizing community tree planting events.

    Environmental Workshops: Hosting educational sessions for schools and communities to raise awareness about environmental issues.

    Recycling Initiatives: Implementing and supporting recycling programs to reduce waste and pollution.

    Clean Energy Advocacy: Promoting and facilitating the adoption of clean and renewable energy sources.

    Wildlife Conservation: Collaborating with local organizations and authorities to protect and preserve regional biodiversity.
